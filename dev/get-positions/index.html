GetPositions

Position Queries​

There are two primary queries available for retrieving Position data:

  • GetPositions: Retrieves a collection of positions based on specified filters and parameters
  • GetPosition: Fetches a single position by its unique identifier (id)

We have additional Position queries in our GraphQL package.

Positions​
