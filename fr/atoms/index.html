Interacting with Atoms

Also referred to as Identities, Atoms are the atomic unit of knowledge in Intuition, representing singular objects or concepts, such as a word, an entity, a meme; any ‘thing’.

There are four distinct ways users can interact with Atoms:

  1. Creating an Atom
  2. Depositing (staking) on an Atom - remember, this is a Signal!
  3. Creating a Triple from three Atoms
  4. Depositing (staking) on an Triple - also a Signal!
